Ambivalent Beats v1.0.0
2012/02/29

To install simply drag-and-drop the 'Ambivalent Beats' folder to the 'Max MIDI Effects' folder in your Live8 Library.

To use, drag-and-drop the Max for Live device, then drag-and-drop the JZML Lemur template on the MFL device.

Also included are 16 drum sounds selected by Ambivalent. You can use these in a Drum Rack.
